USEFUL DETAILS

Questions, answered simply.

Which syllabuses are supported?+

Cambridge IGCSE, Pearson Edexcel IGCSE and Singapore O-Level pathways.

Can students use the platform without tuition?+

Yes. Exam Intelligence is designed as an independent learning workspace and can also complement tutor-led lessons.

Does the AI know future exam questions?+

No. It can analyse permitted historical patterns and suggest revision priorities, but it cannot know future papers.

Are student accounts private?+

The platform is designed with account-level authentication and database rules that isolate each student’s learning records.
