02Is AI assistance included with tuition?+

Exam Intelligence has separate payment and account access, allowing students to choose it alongside one-to-one tuition when useful.

03Does the AI know future exam questions?+

No. It analyses permitted historical patterns and suggests revision priorities, but it cannot know future papers.

The platform uses account authentication and database rules designed to keep each student’s learning records separate.
