Designed Around How Students Actually Learn

AI Engine is a learning platform currently being developed by IGCSEMYSG to support students preparing for IGCSE, Edexcel and O-Level examinations.

The platform is being built around a simple idea: effective learning requires structure, feedback and consistent practice. Our goal is to provide students with tools that help them understand concepts more clearly, identify weaknesses earlier and revise more efficiently.

Personalised Learning

Learning recommendations tailored to individual strengths, weaknesses and academic goals.

Revision Planning

Structured revision tools designed to help students stay organised and consistent.

Progress Insights

Clear visibility into performance trends and areas requiring further attention.

Exam Preparation

Focused support aimed at helping students approach examinations with confidence.

Practice & Feedback

Opportunities for targeted practice supported by meaningful feedback.

Continuous Development

The platform will continue evolving based on student needs and educational research.

Phase 1

Research & Development

Building the foundation, testing concepts and validating learning workflows.

Phase 2

Private Testing

Gathering feedback from a small group of students and refining the experience.

Phase 3

Public Launch

A broader release planned for 2027 with continued improvements thereafter.
